About Us

AviTravel  Network is a travel business platform that lets you start your own travel franchise

and become an IATA-certified independent travel agent, enjoying all the perks of the travel industry!

VIDEO

How It Work?

Joining us allows you to become an independent travel agent. You'll receive an agent login, giving you access to a personalized system for booking travel with any of our suppliers or through our extensive travel portal. By booking travel for yourself or others, you'll earn a commission.

Who will I be partnering with?

You will have access to some of the most powerful partners, such as Royal Caribbean, Carnival, Princess Cruises, Vacation Express, Apple Vacations, American Airlines Vacations, ExpediaTAAP, Disney Destinations, Universal Parks and Resorts Vacations, Centrav and United Airlines, and many other suppliers you likely already use. From USA staycations and day trip tickets to global vacations, we cater to all your needs! You may have booked with some of these partners before, but now you can earn the commission yourself.

Who will I be partnering with?

Anything you book for yourself you can book using one of our amazing partner programmes for preferred exclusive rates or earn commission on your own booking. Should you want to book for anyone else - family, friends, clients - you get paid the commission. What a perfect side hustle and second income stream! Commission is between 10-18% depending on the supplier.
